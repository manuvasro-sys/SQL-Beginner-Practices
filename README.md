# SQL-Beginner-Practices
Starting in the SQLite world
This practice answers the following question:
 1. What are the 10 best-selling video games of all time globally and on which platforms do they belong?
 2. What are the total cumulative global sales for each platform (PS4, XOne, Wii, etc.)? 
 We would like to see a list ranked from most successful to least successful platform.
 3. What are the 5 best-selling video game genres in North America compared to the 5 best-selling genres in Japan?
 4. How many games did Nintendo release per year and what were their total global sales per year?
